Open issue — affects every journey regardless of batch timing
Even if we solved the dead zone entirely and onboarded members the moment they enrolled, the first messages they receive lead with authentication — not brand, not plan basics, not their first debit date or amount. The member is asked to verify their identity before they understand what they signed up for. This is independent of the batch process.
Level 1 EV2 SMS solves this at t=0 for every journey
Payor vs. primary — policy is set  Resolved
Per documentation and agreements, the primary on the plan receives onboarding — not the payor.
Known friction: ~10% of cases  Design consideration
When primary and payor are different people (LLC, spouse, parent as payor) the primary may not have the payor’s verification info needed to complete KBA. Example: daughter is primary, parent is payor — daughter cannot verify parent’s financial details during onboarding.
MVP or Prospect Portal (7–9 screens) is the candidate solution
